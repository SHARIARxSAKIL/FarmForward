Accessible Agricultural AI

FarmForward by SMS.

Our solution is the FarmForward AI SMS Assistant, a simple AI system that works through SMS with numbered options, making it extremely easy to use. When Juma sends a message, he receives a menu, replies with a number, and gets clear practical advice based on weather data, agricultural knowledge, and local conditions.

Works on any phone Numbered menu Tanzania weather + pests + alerts English and Swahili AI assistant option

How Juma uses it

Juma only needs to reply with a number. If he sends 1, he gets a weather forecast and planting advice for Tanzania. If he sends 2, he gets pest recommendations. If he sends 3, he receives storm warnings so he can protect his farm. If he sends 6, he can ask the AI assistant for simple farming guidance in natural language.

Why it matters

FarmForward works on any phone, even the simplest one. That makes it accessible to millions of farmers who currently have no access to digital agricultural tools.
